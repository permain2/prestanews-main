About

We are
The Points Guy

The Points Guy was created for travelers by travelers. We share what we know and love, from the cards in our wallets to the places weve been, because weve seen how points and miles can change lives and we want to help our readers get the most out of every dollar.

What we do

TPG covers topics and creates tools that help our community members travel better, spend smarter and explore further. Our team has flown millions of miles and is respected in the aviation, hotel, cruise, credit card and loyalty industries. We engage, entertain and educate audiences through our written words, videos and social media platforms. TPG also gives back by supporting several charities, including Rainbow Railroad and PeaceJam, in addition to honoring the best in travel each year through our TPG Awards show.

How we got here

The Points Guy was launched in 2010 by Brian Kelly, who leveraged his passion for traveling with points and miles to leave his corporate recruiting job and build a brand that inspires millions of people to travel smarter. Since then, Brian and the TPG team have grown TPG to be a powerhouse travel media platform that reaches a global audience and helps consumers make their travel dreams a reality, one point at a time.

Brians influential work has led him to be recognized on AdWeeks Young Influentials list, labeled Forbes No. 1 travel influencer and included in Travel + Leisures Most Notable People in Travel and Out100s lists. The team he built has been acknowledged with numerous awards including Webbys, Shortys and Lovies for its written, video and social media journalism, plus its social impact work.

We believe
Travel unites us

The Points Guy is committed to using travel as a positive force to bring people together, educate communities and support initiatives to promote equality and opportunity around the globe. TPG gives back millions each year to several charities, including the Make-A-Wish Foundation and Miles4Migrants.

Getting it right

TPG aims to be the source of truth on travel and credit cards. Our experts source information from firsthand experience, industry sources and government documents. Under our high publishing standards, information gathered is fact-checked and vetted by multiple editors for its veracity.

Maximizing every dollar

We give our readers access to expert insights and tools so they can make every dollar count. From booking first-class airfare to earning cash back at the gas pump, we developed our points valuations to help our community determine and invest in the programs that work for them.

What we cover

TPG covers topics that help people travel better, spend smarter and explore further. Our editorial team is ultimately responsible for determining which topics are covered. We maintain the editorial independence that allows us to provide our honest appraisal of services, products and experiences.

  • Credit cards

    We review credit cards based on their benefits, rewards, consumer protections and costs. TPG factors in welcome offers, annual fees, interest rates and the number of free authorized users.

  • Travel

    TPG looks for the hottest trends in travel, focusing on price, accessibility, location, service, loyalty programs, food options and answering the questions travelers are asking.
